# Christy's Creative Direction

## The Spine
Vision: **trustworthy and local**
Direction: **Quietly Confident**

## What Christy Decided
- Vision: Trustworthy and local — a family-run business that's been doing this well for years
- Direction: Quietly Confident — muted slate blue with soft cream and a warm sienna accent
- Typography: Fraunces (refined serif) for headings, DM Sans for body
- Palette: muted slate blue (#3d5a6e) primary, soft cream (#f7f3ee) base, burnt sienna (#b5541c) accent
- Tone: Understated, substance over flash. Lets the properties and trust speak for themselves.
- Layout: Full-page scroll — hero → properties → about → why us → testimonials → contact
- Content: Real listings, real owner backstory (Tom & Linda Vásquez), real testimonials
